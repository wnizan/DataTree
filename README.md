# Data Tree Labs — ML & AI Solutions

**Data Tree Labs** is an Israeli data science, machine learning, and AI consultancy founded by **Nizan Waintraub (ניצן וינטראוב)**.

We help companies and organizations in Israel and worldwide design, build, and deploy intelligent data and AI systems — from early architecture to full production implementation.

- **Data Analysis & Decision Making** — KPI dashboards, trend analysis, business insights, and data-driven optimization
- **Machine Learning Development** — Predictive models, classification, recommendation systems, and anomaly detection
- **AI Implementation for Organizations** — Generative AI integration, intelligent workflow automation, and system connectivity
- **AI Agents & Automation** — Autonomous agentic AI systems connected to CRM, APIs, and LLM pipelines
- **Training & Professional Guidance** — Hands-on ML/AI workshops, mentoring, and AI roadmaps for teams
